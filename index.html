About Us

Incepted in 2023, Tech Chef Ltd is a leading IT Development & Design expert company providing superior Software, Web, Mobile and Creative Designing solutions and services to companies globally. Having a strong Technology Development and Innovation center in Asaba. It is our vision to build digital solutions to satisfy companies across the globe, and also create digitized solutions to the challenges of humanity. TechChef is set to make it’s mark as a well established and sound company driven by the industry’s highly professional and trained individuals and is surely headed in becoming a leading organization which facilitates, enhances and provides measurable business value to customers through most effective uses of technology and Resources to organizations globally.

Our Mission

To enable people and businesses throughout the world realize their full potential, through the digitalization of their products and services

Our Vision

We have the vision to bridge the current Business to customer challenge faced by brands in this present century, by creating digital products to help brands maximize their sales and profit margin by a quantum leap
